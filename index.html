Fender Stratocaster

The guitar that marked a generation

The Fender Stratocaster has existed largely unchanged for 60 years now, allowing it to become an ingrained form in the minds of successive generations.

Rock legend Jimmy Hendrix playing with his Stratocaster.

These are some of the qualities that make the Stratocaster unique:

  • The first Fender guitar to feature three pickups and a spring tension vibrato system.
  • It's double cutaways allowed players easier access to higher positions on the neck.
  • The bridge novel mechanism pivots on a fulcrum design with a six screw bridge plate, allowing the whole set-up to "float" while transferring the strings energy directly into the body.
